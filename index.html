RUNNING CUBE

Steer a cube down a neon road. Weave, jump, double-jump. Grab power-up cubes that grow you, clone you, arm you, or flip gravity — chase the leaderboard in Endless mode or clear the level campaign.

two ways to play
Endless run, or the campaign.
♾️

Endless Mode

Survive as the world scrolls, collect power-up cards, and submit your score to the online PlayFab leaderboard.

🗺️

Level Campaign

Hand-built levels across themed worlds. Survive to the target time to pass — clear with no damage for a Perfect.

❤️

Health & recovery

A heart bar, cherry pickups, and a dizziness mini-game mean a run ends from accumulated damage, not one slip.

🌀

Portals & bosses

Portals teleport you to new themed worlds; a boss scene brings a shooting encounter.
